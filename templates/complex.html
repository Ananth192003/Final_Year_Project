Explore Chemistry Concepts

Atomic Structure

Understanding atoms, electrons, and quantum mechanics.

Electrons orbit the nucleus in quantized energy levels. The quantum model replaces the classical Bohr model for better accuracy.

Chemical Bonding

How atoms form stable molecules.

Ionic bonds involve electron transfer, while covalent bonds share electrons. Hydrogen bonding significantly affects water properties.

Thermodynamics

The energy changes in chemical reactions.

Gibbs free energy determines spontaneity: ΔG = ΔH - TΔS. Negative ΔG means a reaction is spontaneous.

Reaction Kinetics

Speed of reactions and influencing factors.

Catalysts lower activation energy, increasing reaction rates. The Arrhenius equation models temperature dependence.
